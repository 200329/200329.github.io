Featured Brand

Steinway and Sons

Although I do recommend physically going to try pianos, if you are an intense player, or hope to be one, there is one brand that you must have. That, is Steinway and Sons

Steinway was founded March 5th, 1853,starting off in manhattan. An award winning brand, it has patented more than 100 new piano designs. Steinway pianos take up 80% of the high end piano market, and are played by almost all performing concert artists around the world

No one really knows what makes their sound so great, as well as be so responsive. Perhaps it is because each piano is hand-crafted and can take up to a year. Perhaps it is their standards to "sound, touch, beauty, and investment value."

However, because of their quality, Steinways also cost a lot. If you still aren't ready to make that investment, Steinway sells two less expensive brands under the name Boston, and Essex. Boston is a mid-class piano, and Essex is entry level.

Whatever makes this piano better than the others is a hidden secret. But the hand-crafted piano, is one of the best in the industry, and definitely the face of it.
